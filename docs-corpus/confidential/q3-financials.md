# Q3 2025 Financial Summary

**Classification:** CONFIDENTIAL — Board and executive team only
**Period:** July 1 – September 30, 2025

## Revenue

- **Q3 Revenue:** $4.2M (up 28% from Q2's $3.28M)
- **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR):** $15.8M (up 34% year-over-year)
- **Net Revenue Retention:** 118% — existing customers are expanding usage faster than churn

### Revenue Breakdown by Tier

| Tier | Q3 Revenue | % of Total | QoQ Growth |
|------|-----------|------------|------------|
| Enterprise | $2.52M | 60% | +32% |
| Professional | $1.34M | 32% | +24% |
| Starter | $0.34M | 8% | +18% |

Enterprise continues to be our growth engine, driven by three new logos (FinServ Corp, GlobalHealth Inc, TechManufacturing Ltd) that signed multi-year contracts.

## Expenses

- **Total operating expenses:** $3.1M
- **Engineering (salaries + infra):** $1.85M (60% of expenses)
- **Sales & marketing:** $0.72M (23%)
- **G&A:** $0.53M (17%)

### Infrastructure Costs

Cloud infrastructure costs rose to $420K this quarter (up from $340K in Q2), primarily driven by increased LLM API usage as AI agent features gained adoption. We are implementing token budgets and response caching to control this growth.

## Profitability

- **Gross margin:** 74% (target: 75%)
- **Operating margin:** +26% (first positive operating quarter!)
- **Net income:** $1.1M (compared to -$0.2M in Q2)

This is a significant milestone — our first quarter of positive operating income. The improvement is driven by revenue growth outpacing expense growth, particularly in engineering where we deferred three planned hires to Q1 2026.

## Cash Position

- **Cash on hand:** $12.4M
- **Monthly burn rate:** $0 (cash-flow positive this quarter)
- **Runway:** Effectively unlimited at current trajectory (previously 18 months at prior burn rate)

## Forecast

- **Q4 2025 Revenue Forecast:** $4.8M–$5.1M based on signed contracts and pipeline
- **2025 Full-Year Revenue:** $14.5M–$14.8M (original target: $13M)
- **2026 ARR Target:** $24M (assumes continued 34% growth rate plus new product launches)

## Key Risks

1. LLM API cost growth could compress margins if token usage scales faster than revenue
2. Three Enterprise renewals in Q1 2026 worth $1.8M combined — early signals are positive but not yet confirmed
3. Competitive pressure from larger players (Datadog, Splunk) adding similar AI document features
