# Acme Corp is Hiring

We are growing fast and looking for talented people to join our team. Acme Corp offers competitive compensation, remote-first flexibility, and the chance to work on cutting-edge problems at the intersection of AI, security, and enterprise software.

## Open Positions

### Senior Backend Engineer — Distributed Systems

**Location:** Remote (US/Canada) | **Level:** L5–L6

We are looking for an experienced backend engineer to work on our policy evaluation pipeline. You will design and implement low-latency systems that evaluate thousands of access decisions per second. Experience with Go or Rust, distributed consensus, and real-time streaming is highly valued.

**Requirements:** 5+ years backend engineering, experience with distributed systems at scale, strong fundamentals in data structures and algorithms.

### Frontend Engineer — React/TypeScript

**Location:** Remote (US/Canada/UK) | **Level:** L4–L5

Join our frontend team to build the next generation of the DataVault dashboard. You will work with React, TypeScript, Material UI, and real-time data streams (SSE). This role involves close collaboration with our design team and our AI agent orchestration layer.

**Requirements:** 3+ years React/TypeScript experience, familiarity with state management (Zustand, Redux), experience with data visualization.

### Product Manager — AI Features

**Location:** San Francisco or Remote | **Level:** Senior

Own the product roadmap for our AI-powered features, including MCP tool management, agent orchestration, and the security dashboard. You will work directly with engineering, design, and customers to define priorities and ship features that enterprise buyers love.

**Requirements:** 4+ years product management experience, technical background preferred, experience with B2B SaaS.

### Security Engineer

**Location:** Remote (US/Canada) | **Level:** L5

Help us strengthen our security posture. You will conduct internal security reviews, work with our external audit partners, manage our vulnerability disclosure program, and improve our policy engine's rule authoring capabilities.

**Requirements:** 4+ years in application security, experience with penetration testing, familiarity with policy-as-code frameworks.

## Benefits

- Competitive salary and equity
- Full health, dental, and vision insurance
- Unlimited PTO with a 3-week minimum
- $2,500 annual learning budget
- Remote-first with optional co-working stipend
- Annual team offsite

## How to Apply

Send your resume and a brief note about what excites you about Acme Corp to careers@acme-corp.example.com. We review every application and aim to respond within one week.
