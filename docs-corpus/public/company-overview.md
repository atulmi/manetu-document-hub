# Acme Corp: Who We Are

## Our Mission

Acme Corp was founded in 2019 with a single goal: make enterprise data management secure by default. Too many organizations treat security as an afterthought — bolted on after the architecture is set, after the data is flowing, after the first breach. We believe security should be woven into the fabric of every data interaction, every API call, every document access.

## What We Build

Our flagship product, **DataVault**, is a policy-driven data management platform used by over 200 enterprise customers. DataVault provides:

- **Document lifecycle management** — ingest, classify, store, and retire documents with full audit trails
- **Role-based access control** — powered by our Manetu Policy Engine (MPE), which evaluates every access request in real time
- **AI-assisted workflows** — using the Model Context Protocol (MCP) to let AI agents interact with documents while respecting organizational policies

We also offer **DataVault Cloud**, a managed SaaS version that handles infrastructure, compliance, and scaling so teams can focus on their data rather than their servers.

## The Team

Acme Corp is a team of 85 people distributed across San Francisco, Toronto, and London. Our engineering team of 42 includes specialists in distributed systems, security, machine learning, and developer experience. We are proudly bootstrapped through our first three years and closed our Series A in early 2024.

## Our Values

- **Security first** — every feature ships with policy controls
- **Transparency** — our public security FAQ and audit reports are available to all customers
- **Developer experience** — we build tools that engineers actually want to use
